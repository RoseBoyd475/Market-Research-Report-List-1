<p><p>BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) security encompasses various market types to protect organizational data on personal devices. Mobile Device Management (MDM) focuses on securing and managing devices through policies and remote actions, ensuring compliance. Mobile Application Management (MAM) controls access and usage of applications on devices, allowing selective security measures without overreaching device control. Mobile Content Management (MCM) safeguards and manages corporate content accessed on personal devices, ensuring data integrity and security while supporting user productivity. Together, they create a comprehensive BYOD security strategy.</p></p>
