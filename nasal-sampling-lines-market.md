<p><p>Nasal sampling lines are crucial in respiratory therapy, facilitating gas sampling from patients. The market is divided into two types: low-flow and high-flow nasal sampling lines. Low-flow nasal sampling lines are designed for minimal respiratory support, ideal for stable patients, while high-flow nasal sampling lines provide enhanced oxygen delivery and can accommodate higher oxygen flow rates, catering to patients with more severe respiratory needs.
