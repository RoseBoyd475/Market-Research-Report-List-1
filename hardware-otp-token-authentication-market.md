<p><p>The hardware OTP token authentication market is segmented into USB tokens, SIM tokens, and mini tokens. USB tokens connect directly to devices via USB ports, providing secure access through one-time passwords. SIM tokens, integrated into mobile devices or SIM cards, generate OTPs for authentication during online transactions or app access. Mini tokens are compact, portable devices that generate OTPs independently, often featuring a display. Each type offers varying convenience and security levels, catering to diverse user needs in authentication processes.</p></p>
