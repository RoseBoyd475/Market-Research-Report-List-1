<p><p>The Magnetic Bullet Separator market is divided into two main types: Electromagnet and Permanent Magnet. Electromagnetic separators utilize electrical current to generate a magnetic field, allowing for adjustable strength based on specific separation needs, making them suitable for dynamic environments. In contrast, Permanent Magnet separators rely on fixed magnetic fields generated by permanent magnets, offering a cost-effective solution with lower maintenance requirements. Both types serve diverse industries, including mining, recycling, and food processing, ensuring efficient metal contamination control.</p></p>
