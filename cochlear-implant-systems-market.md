<p><strong>The Cochlear Implant Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unilateral</li><li>Binaural</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cochlear implant systems are categorized into unilateral and binaural markets based on the number of devices used. The unilateral market involves the implantation of a single device, typically suited for patients with hearing loss in one ear, providing them with amplified sound and improved auditory perception. In contrast, the binaural market includes the use of two implants, which helps in better sound localization, improved hearing in noisy environments, and enhanced overall auditory experience for patients with bilateral hearing loss.</p></p>
