<p><p>The cooled thermal infrared detector market primarily features two types: Vanadium Oxide (VOx) and Amorphous Silicon (A-Si). VOx detectors, known for their high sensitivity and wide dynamic range, are commonly used in military and industrial applications. In contrast, A-Si detectors offer cost-effectiveness and lower power consumption, making them suitable for commercial and consumer applications. Both types play crucial roles in various sectors, including surveillance, aerospace, and medical imaging, leveraging their distinct advantages to meet diverse detection needs.</p></p>
