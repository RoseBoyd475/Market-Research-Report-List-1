<p><p>The palletizer market consists of two main types: traditional palletizers and robotic palletizers. Traditional palletizers utilize mechanical systems to stack products onto pallets in a fixed pattern, often providing high throughput and efficiency for predictable loads. In contrast, robotic palletizers employ robotic arms to automate the stacking process, offering greater flexibility and adaptability to varied product shapes and sizes. Both types serve essential roles in automating warehousing and distribution processes, enhancing operational efficiency and reducing labor costs.</p></p>
