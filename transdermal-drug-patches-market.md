<p><p>Transdermal drug patches are adhesive patches applied to the skin, designed to deliver medication through the dermal layers into systemic circulation. Commonly used for hormones, pain relief, and smoking cessation, these patches enhance patient compliance by reducing the frequency of medication intake.</p><p>The Transdermal Drug Patches Market is experiencing significant growth due to rising chronic diseases, an increasing geriatric population, and a greater focus on drug delivery systems that reduce the need for needles. Furthermore, the trend toward personalized medicine is facilitating the development of patches tailored to individual patient needs.</p><p>Geographically, North America is a leading market, fueled by advanced healthcare infrastructure and robust research activities.
