<p><p>Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy (CRT) Devices are advanced therapeutic tools designed to improve the efficiency of the heart's pumping action in patients with heart failure, particularly those with ventricular synchrony issues. Factors such as an aging population, lifestyle changes, and the growing incidence of obesity and diabetes are contributing to the demand for CRT.
