<p><p>The HVAC terminal units market encompasses three main types: single duct, dual duct, and fan-powered units. Single duct units supply conditioned air through a single duct, maintaining simplicity and efficiency. Dual duct units combine hot and cold air from separate ducts, allowing for precise temperature control in different spaces. Fan-powered units use an integrated fan to enhance air distribution and comfort, making them ideal for variable load spaces. Each type serves unique needs in climate control and energy efficiency.</p></p>
