<p><p>Barrier shrink films encompass various types used for packaging and preservation. Flowpacks are efficient for sealing products in upright or horizontal orientations, while vacuum bags enhance shelf life by removing air. Shrink forms are pre-shaped films that mold to products upon heat application. Chubs are cylindrical packaging ideal for processed meats and cheeses, and shrink wraps provide versatile covering for a range of products, ensuring protection and freshness. Each type plays a vital role in food safety and longevity.</p></p>
