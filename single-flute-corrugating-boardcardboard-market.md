<p><p>Single flute corrugating board/cardboard is a type of packaging material characterized by a single wave layer (or flute) that is sandwiched between two liner sheets. This design provides a lightweight yet sturdy option for packaging, making it particularly suitable for various industries, including food and beverage, e-commerce, and consumer goods. The main advantage of single flute cardboard is its cost-effectiveness and lower material usage while still maintaining adequate strength for protective packaging.</p><p>The Single Flute Corrugating Board/Cardboard Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.1% during the forecast period.
