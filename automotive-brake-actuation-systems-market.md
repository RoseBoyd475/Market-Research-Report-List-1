<p><p>The automotive brake actuation systems market encompasses key components like brake boosters, tandem master cylinders (TMCs), and brake fluid reservoirs. Brake boosters enhance braking efficiency by amplifying the driver's force, while tandem master cylinders ensure consistent brake force distribution across multiple wheels. Brake fluid reservoirs store hydraulic fluid, essential for activating the braking system. Together, these components play a critical role in vehicle safety and performance, driving innovations in braking technology to meet growing automotive demands.</p></p>
