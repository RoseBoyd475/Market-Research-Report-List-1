<p><strong>The Photonic IR Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave Infrared</li><li>Mid Wave Infrared</li><li>Long Wave Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photonic IR Sensor market is divided into three categories based on wavelength: Short Wave Infrared (SWIR), Mid Wave Infrared (MWIR), and Long Wave Infrared (LWIR). SWIR sensors operate in the 0.9 to 1.7 µm range, offering high sensitivity for applications like telecommunications and material analysis. MWIR, covering 3 to 5 µm, is used in thermal imaging and surveillance. LWIR sensors, ranging from 8 to 14 µm, excel in heat detection and environmental monitoring, catering to industrial and military applications.</p></p>
