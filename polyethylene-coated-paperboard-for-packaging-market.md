<p><p>Polyethylene coated paperboard is used in packaging to enhance resistance to moisture, grease, and contaminants. Two primary types are low-density polyethylene (LDPE) and high-density polyethylene (HDPE). LDPE is flexible, providing a soft touch and good sealability, making it ideal for items needing tight packaging. HDPE is sturdier, offering superior strength and chemical resistance, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types contribute to extending product shelf life and are widely used in food and beverage packaging, as well as consumer goods.</p></p>
