<p><ul><li>Fixed Cutter</li><li>Roller Cone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas and oil drill bits market consists of two primary types: fixed cutter bits and roller cone bits. Fixed cutter bits, commonly made from materials like diamond or tungsten carbide, utilize a sharp cutting edge to penetrate rock formations and provide excellent performance in hard rock drilling. Roller cone bits feature rotating cones with teeth, allowing them to effectively break down various types of substrates. Both types are essential for optimizing drilling efficiency and maintaining wellbore stability in hydrocarbon exploration.</p></p>
