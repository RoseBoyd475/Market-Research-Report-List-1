<p><p>The Iron Deficiency Anemia Therapy Market encompasses various treatment modalities. Oral iron therapy is the most common, utilizing supplements to restore iron levels, suitable for mild cases. Parenteral iron therapy involves intravenous or intramuscular infusions for patients who cannot absorb oral iron or need rapid replenishment. Red blood cell transfusions are reserved for severe anemia, quickly increasing hemoglobin levels. Additional therapies may include iron-rich diet adjustments and erythropoiesis-stimulating agents to enhance red blood cell production.</p></p>
