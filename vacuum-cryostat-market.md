<p><strong>The Vacuum Cryostat Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Helium-flow</li><li>Liquid Helium</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum cryostat market primarily consists of two types: helium-flow cryostats and liquid helium cryostats. Helium-flow cryostats utilize a continuous flow of gaseous helium to maintain low temperatures, allowing for precise thermal control and minimizing liquid handling. In contrast, liquid helium cryostats rely on the storage of liquid helium for cooling, providing ultra-low temperatures essential for certain scientific applications. Both types are critical in research, medical imaging, and various industrial processes requiring cryogenic conditions.</p></p>
