<p><p>The dry dairy powder market encompasses several types, each defined by fat content. Low fat dry dairy powder has reduced fat levels, making it a healthier option while still maintaining some creaminess. Skimmed dry dairy powder contains minimal fat, offering a lower-calorie alternative. These powders are widely used in food processing, baking, and nutritional products for their convenience and shelf stability.</p></p>
