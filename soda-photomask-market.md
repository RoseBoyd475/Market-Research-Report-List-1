<p><strong>The Soda Photomask Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Soda Chrome Mask</li><li>Soda Emulsion Mask</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Soda Photomask market encompasses two primary types: Soda Chrome Masks and Soda Emulsion Masks. Soda Chrome Masks are made using a thin layer of chromium on a glass substrate, offering high precision in photolithography for semiconductor applications. In contrast, Soda Emulsion Masks utilize a photosensitive emulsion to create patterns, providing flexibility and ease of use in various applications, including printed circuit boards and MEMS. Both types are essential for accurately transferring designs onto substrates in the manufacturing process.</p></p>
