<p><p>Fluoride-based fouling release coatings are categorized into two main market types: biocide-containing and biocide-free. Biocide-containing coatings incorporate active substances that prevent the growth of marine organisms on surfaces, enhancing longevity and effectiveness in harsh environments. In contrast, biocide-free coatings rely on non-toxic, physical properties to create a slippery surface, discouraging foulant attachment without harmful chemicals. Both types aim to reduce maintenance costs and improve performance in marine and industrial applications while addressing environmental concerns.</p></p>
