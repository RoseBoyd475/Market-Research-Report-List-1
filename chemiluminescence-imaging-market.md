<p><p>The Chemiluminescence Imaging Market is segmented into various types based on the source of light emission. Chemical luminescence relies on chemical reactions for light production. Biochemical luminescence, derived from biological processes, is often seen in organisms like fireflies. Electroluminescence involves light emission from materials in response to an electric current, while photoluminescence occurs when materials absorb photons and re-emit them. Each type has unique applications in fields such as biology, diagnostics, and environmental monitoring, highlighting their diverse utility in imaging technologies.</p></p>
