<p><p>The air operated aluminum diaphragm pump market is categorized into two main types: single acting and double acting pumps. Single acting pumps utilize one diaphragm to create a suction and discharge cycle with each stroke, making them suitable for lower flow applications. In contrast, double acting pumps have two diaphragms working simultaneously, delivering a continuous flow and higher output, making them ideal for demanding industrial applications. Both types leverage compressed air to operate, providing efficient fluid transfer solutions.</p></p>
