<p><p>The pipette tips market can be categorized into two main types: non-filtered and filtered pipette tips. Non-filtered pipette tips are designed for general liquid handling tasks, providing cost-effective solutions for routine applications. In contrast, filtered pipette tips are equipped with a barrier that prevents aerosols and contaminants from entering the pipette, making them ideal for sensitive assays, ensuring sample integrity, and reducing the risk of cross-contamination. Both types serve distinct needs in laboratories and research settings.</p></p>
