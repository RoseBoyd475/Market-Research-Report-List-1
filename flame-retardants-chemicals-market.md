<p><p>The flame retardants chemicals market can be categorized into two main types: non-halogenated and halogenated flame retardants. Non-halogenated flame retardants, derived from organic or inorganic compounds, are considered safer alternatives, as they reduce environmental impact and health risks. In contrast, halogenated flame retardants contain bromine or chlorine, offering effective flame resistance but raising concerns due to potential toxicity and environmental persistence. Both types serve critical roles in enhancing fire safety across various applications, from construction to electronics.</p></p>
