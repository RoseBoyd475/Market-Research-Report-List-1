<p><p>The Turbine Inlet Cooling System market consists of three primary types: </p><p>1. **Inlet Fogging**: This technique uses fine water mist to cool the air entering the turbine, increasing efficiency and power output.  </p><p>2. **Chiller Systems**: These systems utilize refrigeration technology to lower air temperature before it reaches the turbine, enhancing performance under high-temperature conditions.  </p><p>3. **Evaporative Cooling**: This method leverages water evaporation to cool the incoming air, providing a cost-effective solution with significant efficiency improvements in hot climates.</p></p>
