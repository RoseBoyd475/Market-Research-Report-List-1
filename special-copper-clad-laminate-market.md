<p><p>The Special Copper Clad Laminate market includes various types tailored for specific applications. BT/epoxy fiberglass cloth boards offer excellent thermal and electrical properties. Modified FR-4 boasts low Coefficient of Thermal Expansion (CTE) and low dielectric constant/dissipation factor for high-frequency use. PPO modified epoxy boards provide enhanced thermal and mechanical performance. BT-like boards serve as cost-effective alternatives. PTFE boards are ideal for high-frequency applications, while hydrocarbon boards offer unique chemical resistance. PI/glass fiber cloth boards exhibit high thermal stability, with other specialty boards addressing niche requirements.</p></p>
