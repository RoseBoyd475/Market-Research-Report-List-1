<p><ul><li>Dry Plasma Thawer</li><li>Wet Plasma Thawer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plasma thawer systems are essential in blood banks and medical facilities for rapidly and efficiently thawing frozen plasma. The market includes two main types: dry plasma thawers and wet plasma thawers. Dry plasma thawers use air convection to gently warm plasma bags, ensuring uniform thawing without water, reducing contamination risk. In contrast, wet plasma thawers immerse plasma bags in a heated water bath, offering rapid thawing but increasing the potential for contamination. Each type caters to specific facility needs and operational preferences.</p></p>
