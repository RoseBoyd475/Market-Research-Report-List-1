<p><ul><li>Contact Sensing</li><li>Non-Contact Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT Smart Temperature Sensors market is divided into two main types: contact sensing and non-contact sensing. Contact sensing involves sensors that require physical contact with the object to measure temperature, providing accurate readings for applications like HVAC and industrial processes. In contrast, non-contact sensing uses infrared technology to detect temperature from a distance, ideal for measuring moving objects or in environments where direct contact is impossible. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing real-time monitoring and automation across various sectors.</p></p>
