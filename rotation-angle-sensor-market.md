<p><p>The rotation angle sensor market is categorized into two main types: brushless sensors and brushed sensors. Brushless sensors utilize electromagnetic principles without physical contact, resulting in higher efficiency, longevity, and reduced wear. They are ideal for high-precision applications and harsh environments. In contrast, brushed sensors rely on physical contact between brushes and a rotating component, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness but with more maintenance needs and shorter lifespan. Both types serve various industries, including automotive, robotics, and industrial automation.</p></p>
