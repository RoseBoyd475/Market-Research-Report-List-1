<p><ul><li>Saline IV Flush Syringe</li><li>Heparin IV Flush Syringe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The prefilled IV flush syringe market includes saline and heparin types, each serving distinct clinical purposes. Saline IV flush syringes are primarily used to maintain venous access and prevent clotting by flushing intravenous lines with sterile saline solution. Heparin IV flush syringes, containing anticoagulant heparin, are utilized to prevent thrombus formation in central venous catheters and maintain patency in hemodialysis. Both types enhance efficiency and safety in healthcare settings, reducing preparation time and minimizing contamination risks.</p></p>
