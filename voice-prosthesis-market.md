<p><strong>The Voice Prosthesis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indwelling Voice Prosthesis</li><li>Non-indwelling Voice Prosthesis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The voice prosthesis market comprises two main types: indwelling and non-indwelling voice prostheses. Indwelling voice prostheses are implanted surgically within the tracheoesophageal puncture (TEP) and are designed for long-term use, providing a permanent solution for individuals requiring voice rehabilitation after surgery, such as laryngectomy. In contrast, non-indwelling voice prostheses are removable devices that can be easily inserted and taken out, offering flexibility for users who may prefer temporary solutions or need different devices for varying situations. Both types aim to restore vocal communication effectively.</p></p>
