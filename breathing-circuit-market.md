<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Breathing Circuit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open</li><li>Semi-Open</li><li>Closed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Breathing circuits are categorized into three types based on their gas handling: </p><p>1. **Open Circuit**: In this system, exhaled gas is vented to the atmosphere, with fresh gas supplied during inhalation. </p><p>2. **Semi-Open Circuit**: This combines elements of open and closed systems, where some exhaled gas is re-circulated after carbon dioxide removal, and some is discharged to the environment. </p><p>3. **Closed Circuit**: Here, exhaled gases are fully recirculated, with only minimal fresh gas added, providing efficient gas management and reducing wastage.</p></p>
