<p><strong>The Automobile Exhaust Catalyst Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Three-way Catalyst (TWC)</li><li>Four-Way Catalyst (4WC)</li><li>Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC)</li><li>Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) Catalyst</li><li>Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Catalyst</li><li>Ammonia Slip Catalyst (ASC)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile exhaust catalyst market encompasses various types of catalysts designed to reduce harmful emissions. Three-way catalysts (TWC) effectively convert hydrocarbons, carbon monoxide, and nitrogen oxides in gasoline engines. Four-way catalysts (4WC) add another layer by addressing additional pollutants. Diesel oxidation catalysts (DOC) focus on light-duty diesel engines, while selective catalytic reduction (SCR) catalysts reduce nitrogen oxide emissions using ammonia. Diesel particulate filters (DPF) capture soot and particulate matter. Ammonia slip catalysts (ASC) help control ammonia emissions, with other specialized catalysts addressing unique emission challenges across different vehicles.</p></p>
