<p><p>Invasive blood pressure monitoring sensors are categorized into single, dual, and triple channel sensors based on their functionality. Single channel sensors are designed to measure blood pressure from one site, providing a straightforward approach. Dual channel sensors allow simultaneous monitoring from two sites, enhancing accuracy and responsiveness. Triple channel sensors offer the capability to monitor three different sites, providing comprehensive data for critical care management. This segmentation caters to varying clinical needs, influencing device selection in healthcare settings.</p></p>
