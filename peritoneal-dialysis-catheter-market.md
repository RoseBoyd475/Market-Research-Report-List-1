<p><p>The Peritoneal Dialysis Catheter market consists of two primary types: straight catheters and pigtail-curled catheters. Straight catheters feature a simple, linear design that allows for easy insertion and effective fluid exchange, making them a common choice for patients. Pigtail-curled catheters, on the other hand, have a coil-like design that enhances retention within the peritoneal cavity, reducing the risk of displacement. Both types serve specific patient needs, facilitating efficient dialysis procedures and improving overall treatment outcomes.</p></p>
